Barritt's Butchery

14/07/21
Brothers Bulldogs would like to thank silver sponsor for the past two seasons, Barritt’s Butchery.
Barritt's Butchery is a family-run traditional butchers shop, established in 2005 by Des and Nicole Barritt to provide quality meat, local produce and service with a smile, to the people of Bundaberg. They believe in giving customers nothing but the finest quality products and friendly, expert service to match. Barritt’s Butchery offers beautiful, fresh, local, and delicious cuts of the quality assured meats you love such as:
• Beef (steaks, brisket, sausages, joints, cheek, mince)
• Lamb (Chops, joints, steaks, loins, legs, shoulder, mince)
• Chicken (Whole legs, breast fillets, thighs, wings)
• Pork (Joints, chops, loins, sausages, legs, cheeks, steaks, fillets, ribs)
• Small Goods
If you’d like more information on any of the products mentioned, or to check out their range of specialty meats head over to the Barritt’s Butchery website: https://www.barrittsbutchery.com.au

Ross Gray Motor City

05/06/21
Brothers Bulldogs would like to thank 2021 Silver Sponsor and long-term supporter of the club Ross Gray Motor City.

After joining the mighty Bulldogs in 1977, Ross Gray played over 100 senior games including the 1980, 86 & 87 Premierships and has supported the club in one way or another ever since. Former players Mark Mason & Neil Hempseed both echoed the sentiments that Ross was as hard as nails on the field and a loyal team man off it with Mason very happy he played alongside, rather than against him.
For more than 32 years the friendly staff at Ross Gray Motor City have been selling and servicing new and used vehicles in Bundaberg and the surrounding areas.

Their friendly staff are specialists in
- Mazda
- Mitsubishi
- GMSV
- MG Motors
- Finance & Insurance
- Quality Used Cars
- Spare parts

Ross Gray Motor City enhances your buying experience by providing expert after sales service with courtesy pick-up and drop-off convenience, knowing your car is being treated with the care and professionalism you would expect in their large modern workshop service area.
For more information or to browse their range of new and used vehicles visit the Ross Gray website - www.rossgray.com.au

South Bundy Buses

26/05/21 | Brothers Bulldogs would like to thank 2021 Silver Sponsor, and long-term supporter of the club Richard and Fiona Hayes from South Bundy Buses.
Starting in 1998 with the takeover of five contracted school bus services which operated from Childers Road, Pine Creek and South Bingera into Bundaberg schools, South Bundy Buses have now expanded to a fleet of 14 vehicles covering the Bundaberg and Childers regions.
During this time they have regularly supported numerous events across Queensland and Australia with the highlights being the 2000 Sydney Olympics and Paralympic Games, and 2018 Gold Coast Commonwealth Games where they transported numerous athletes and officials.
Today, South Bundy Buses continues to strive for improvement in quality standards to provide safe, reliable services. Further, they are a proudly local and family operated business with strong ties to the local community through their involvement with local schools, local business and the sporting community.

Important Season Information

15/03/2020 | Today the Wide Bay League advised Brothers Bulldogs that the 2020 AFL Wide Bay Community season is indefinitely postponed due to the current circumstances surrounding the corona virus. At this stage they are hopeful that the Womens competition can still be completed, however, the beginning of the other competitions has been postponed. At this stage we are still able to train, and the league is hopeful that this postponement will only last for a limited time, however, there is a lot of uncertainty surrounding the circumstances. We are hopeful to know more by Tuesday training, and the clubs will be updated regularly, but there certainly won’t be any footy action prior to Easter. At this stage I am unsure of whether Auskick will continue on Friday nights, but will relay this information as it comes to hand.
Ash Charlesworth - Junior Vice President
